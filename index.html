About

GI Explorer is a desktop map application that allows users to explore the green infrastructure of urban places.

With GI Explorer, users can:

  • View a variety of green infrastructure data, including parks, trails, and green roofs
  • Explore green infrastructure data in 3D
  • Create custom maps of green infrastructure

GI Explorer is a free and open-source application.
